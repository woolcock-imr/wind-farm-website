Functional Outcomes of Sleep Questionnaire (FOSQ)

Note: In this questionnaire the words "sleepy" or "tired" are used, it describes the feeling that you can't keep your eyes open, your head is droopy, that you want to nod off or that you feel the urge to take a nap. These words do not refer to the tired or fatigued feeling you may have after you have exercised.

FOSQ questions are answered using numbers from 0-4.

• 0 = I don't do this activity for other reasons
• 1=Yes, extreme
• 2=Yes, moderate
• 3=Yes, a little
• 4=No
